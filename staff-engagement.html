The Show

Four Formats. One Night to Remember.

Every Staff Engagement show is built around these four formats — each one designed for a different kind of energy, a different kind of moment, and a different kind of reaction from the room.

01

Trivia Feud

We surveyed 100 people — teams compete to match the most popular answers, not the correct ones. The host reveals answers one by one as teams rack up matches. Strategy over knowledge. The best round for generating crowd noise.

02

Bingo

Every player gets a personalised bingo card on their phone. Questions or music clips are called and players mark off matches in real time. First to complete a line calls it. Works as trivia, music, or a mix of both.

03

Fastest Finger

A question goes up, buzzers go live, first to hit theirs gets to answer. A wrong answer hands the question to your opponents — so certainty matters as much as speed. The format that produces the most electric single moments of the show.

04

Word Games

A Wheel of Fortune-style format played on screen. Hidden words are revealed letter by letter — teams race to solve before the board is complete. Anagrams, word searches, and phrase reveals all feature. Rewards pattern recognition over trivia recall.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the evening run?

+

The show itself runs approximately 90 minutes. Gaming stations can run before and after for as long as you need. A typical Staff Engagement evening runs three to four hours in total, including arrival, the show, and post-show gaming time.

What does the venue need to provide?

+

A screen or projector, power sources, and enough floor space for the gaming stations and team tables. We bring everything else — all equipment, buzzers, laptop, speakers, and content. For DJ bookings, a reasonable acoustic space and a power source are all that is needed.

Can we choose the music for the DJ?

+

Yes. We take a brief before the event covering preferred genres, any tracks you want included, and anything you want to avoid. The DJ is fully briefed on the tone and format of the evening before arrival.

Can we have gaming stations without the show?

+

No. Gaming stations and the DJ are included as part of the full Staff Engagement package. The show is always the centrepiece, and the gaming stations and DJ run alongside it to create the complete evening experience.

How far in advance should we book?

+

We recommend 4–6 weeks. For larger groups of 100 or more, 8 weeks gives us enough time to prepare the right content. November and December dates fill fastest — if you are planning a Christmas party, get in touch as early as possible.

What is a typical total cost for a Staff Engagement evening?

+

Gaming stations and a resident DJ are included in every Staff Engagement package. A group of 60 people would cost £2,250 — everything included. Every booking is quoted individually — send us your date and group size and we will confirm the exact figure within one working day.
