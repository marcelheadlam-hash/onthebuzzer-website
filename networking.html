The Problem

People in the Same Room Who Have Never Spoken.

Most networking events put people in a room, hand them a drink, and hope for the best. The conversations that happen are the same ones that always happen — and the people who don't know anyone leave having still not met anyone.

Our Networking format changes the dynamic. Light-touch software and gaming equipment are placed around the room. People engage with what catches their eye, find themselves competing with someone they have never met, and suddenly have a real reason to talk.

There is no host on stage, no structured programme, no awkward icebreakers. The format creates the conditions. The connections happen naturally.

The Software

Five Formats That Start Conversations.

Our software runs on screens around the room. People drift toward what interests them, find themselves competing with someone they have never met, and suddenly have something to talk about.

01

Speed Trivia

Teams buzz in on colour-coded buzzers — faster answers score more points, but one wrong answer ends the round. The live leaderboard is visible to the whole room, keeping energy high throughout. Each round lasts around 45 seconds, people can rotate in and out freely, and no host is needed. Works without audio so it never competes with your venue atmosphere. Easy questions, competitive format — it is about speed, not general knowledge.

02

Trivia Feud

We surveyed 100 people — teams compete to match the most popular answers, not the correct ones. Answers are revealed one by one as teams rack up matches. Strategy over knowledge. The format that generates the most debate between teammates.

03

Bingo

Every player gets a personalised bingo card on their phone. Questions or music clips are called and teams mark off matches in real time. First team to complete a line wins the round. Works as trivia, music, or a mix of both.

04

Fastest Finger

A question goes up, buzzers go live, first team to buzz in gets to answer. A wrong answer hands the question to opponents — so certainty matters as much as speed. The format that produces the most electric individual moments of the evening.

05

Word Games

A Wheel of Fortune-style format on screen. Hidden words reveal letter by letter — teams race to solve before the board is complete. Anagrams, word searches, and phrase reveals all feature. Rewards pattern recognition over trivia recall.
