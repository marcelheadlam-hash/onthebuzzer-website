Giving Back

Competition as a
Force for Good.

On The Buzzer uses the energy of competition to raise money for causes that matter. Every event is an opportunity to give back.

The Philosophy

Games Are the Most Powerful Tool for Raising Money.

We believe businesses have a responsibility to give back to the communities they operate in. For On The Buzzer, that is not a policy — it is built into why we exist.

Competition creates presence. When people are genuinely competing, they are fully in the room — and that is the environment in which generosity happens naturally. A competitive format holds attention in a way that a speaker or a raffle never does. Giving becomes part of a shared experience rather than a separate ask.

In 2025 our Movember fundraising event raised over £3,000 for men's health. This is only the beginning.
