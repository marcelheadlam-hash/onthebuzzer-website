The Format

Why A Gameshow

Every failed corporate event has the same root cause. The format asked too much of the people in the room. Gameshows work because competition is a universal language. It does not require anyone to open up, share, or perform on demand. The structure creates the energy. The rounds create the moments. People engage because they want to — not because they have been told to.

Inclusive by Design

The Format Is Fair.
That Changes Everything.

Seniority is irrelevant. Whether you are the CEO or a new starter, speed on the buzzer and strategic wit are the only currencies that matter. The format levels the room without anyone having to be told to participate.

The Show

Six rounds available. We select three.

Every booking is different. Before your event, we review your group and choose the three rounds best suited to your team, your culture, and your objectives.

01

The Sprint

Rapid-fire questions. First to buzz in answers — no conferring, no second chances.

02

Delegation

Teams nominate one person to answer for everyone. It reveals who the room actually trusts.

03

The Collective

Every team member must agree before an answer is submitted. Consensus under pressure.

04

The Wager

Teams stake their accumulated points on a single answer. Go big and get it wrong and the game changes completely.

05

The Consensus

Predict what the majority of the room will say. There is no right answer — only the most popular one.

06

The Board

The finale. Teams go head to head on the most popular survey answers. Get it wrong and hand the game to your opponents.
