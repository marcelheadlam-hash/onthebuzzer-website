Our Story

Built on a Simple Idea.
Competition Connects People.

On The Buzzer exists because Marcel understood something most event formats miss. The fastest way to get people genuinely engaged with each other is to give them something to compete over.

The Insight

Competition Is the Shortcut to Connection.

Nobody checks their phone during a penalty shootout. Nobody holds back when there is a buzzer on the table and points on the line. Competition removes the social friction that makes most corporate events feel forced.

When people are competing, they stop performing. They react honestly. They celebrate together, commiserate together, and argue over answers together. Those are the moments that build real relationships — and they happen naturally, without any facilitation required.

Marcel built The Everyone Show around this principle. Give people a reason to compete and connection follows automatically. That is the foundation of everything On The Buzzer does.

The format does not manufacture connection. It creates the conditions for it to happen on its own.
